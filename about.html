Character Background

Born Claire Farron, "Lightning" is the main protagonist in Final Fantasy VIII. She tries to save her younger sister, Serah, who was turned into a I'Clie, only to become one herself. Lightning is a determined and intense character with a cold, standoffish personality.

About Us

This website is a fansite for Lightning Farron, the main protagonist of a multi-level fantasy video game, Final Fantasy VIII-Lightning Returns. Fans can come here and read, view, share, comment on everything Lightning.
